About

Brief bio

I am an integrated M.S./Ph.D. student in Information and Communication Engineering at Chungbuk National University.

My research focuses on recommender systems, particularly inductive recommendation, temporal graph learning, and event-based social networks.

I study how recommendation models can remain effective in dynamic environments where new users, newly created items, and evolving interaction patterns continuously emerge.

My recent work explores spatiotemporal graph neural networks for event recommendation under strict cold-start settings.
